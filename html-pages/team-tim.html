Get to Know Tim

Tim Hefty, CPA has been providing financial and managerial consulting services to individuals and businesses for over 30 years. At our firm, we believe in a relationship that goes beyond the numbers. This means we strive to understand your personal, professional, and family goals as we tailor our advice to meet your financial needs.

Tim is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA), Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE) and Certified in Financial Forensics (CFF) as well as a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA), Association of Certified Fraud Examiners (ACFE) and Ohio Society of Certified Public Accountants (OSCPA).

Tim resides in Canfield, Ohio with his wife Elise, his office manager and business partner. They enjoy playing golf and gardening in their spare time.
