Total Wealth Management

A concierge approach to every dimension of your financial life

The Total Wealth Management Relationship

For those that have accumulated sizable assets, business owners, or are high income earners, managing the many aspects of your complete financial picture is complex. Total Wealth Management is designed for those that appreciate a concierge approach to addressing these complexities and the required integration of all professionals that is essential, such as income tax planning, investments, insurance, banking, and more.

Some of the Many Annual Deliverables

With a Total Wealth Management Relationship — a Spring and Fall Total Wealth review meeting. With each of these every-180-day interactions, clients receive:

  • Hand-curated 4–5 page “Summary of Recommendations” which highlight all of our best thinking and specific action items that our team will be taking on your behalf.
  • Full detailed 65-page financial plan with cash-flow analysis, retirement projections, investment strategy, tax analysis, estate planning analysis, and insurance analysis.
  • CPA, insurance agents, and estate planning attorneys integrated into meetings.
  • Annual tax preparation and tax planning, provided by one of the several network CPAs of The Affinity Group.
  • Assigned Senior Wealth Advisor along with dedicated Relationship Manager and two Financial Planners.
  • Unlimited and ongoing coordination/collaboration with our or the client’s attorneys, insurance specialists, and banking professionals.
  • eMoney personal financial website — providing real-time connectivity and updating your total financial picture (investments, spending, goal tracking, tax analysis) daily.
  • No per-engagement interaction costs. Calls, emails, screenshare, or face-to-face meetings are always available as needed outside of Spring and Fall Review Meetings.

TWM monthly retainers begin at $350 per month. Pricing is based upon net-worth, complexity, and investment management assets.

Total Wealth Management Explained

How It Works

Onboarding Process

Discovery

Who you are and what’s most important to you is where it all begins. Our firm’s Senior Wealth Advisor Scott Lowe sits down for a discovery session. We want to understand who you are and what’s most important to you. Personal financial planning must be personal.

Recommendations & Specialists

Our team delivers the completed financial plan as well as summary recommendations. Outside specialists join this meeting to provide a second opinion in the areas of tax planning and estate planning. In 60 days following the Recommendation meeting our team rolls up our sleeves and quarterbacks the implementation of the agreed-upon recommendations.

Ongoing Process

Relationship

Proper planning is not a single event — it’s an ongoing, never-ending process. Clients will have a wealth plan review every spring and every fall, with access to anytime communication in between these meetings.

Technology

Although we are located in Canton, Ohio, as a Fiduciary financial advisor we serve clients across the nation with the same personal touch as clients down the street because of the world-class technology we incorporate. Every Total Wealth Management client is provided access to a personal, private, secure, and all-encompassing financial website — a living, breathing GPS that aggregates all your personal data with daily updates.

For clients who prefer a more singular focus on their investment planning, Total Wealth Management is not suitable and they should consider our Investment-only offering.
