What It Is

The Total Wealth Management Relationship

“Success brings complexity. Total Wealth Management is designed to own that complexity on your behalf.”

For those who have accumulated sizable assets, own a business, or are high-income earners, managing the many moving parts of your complete financial picture is genuinely complex. Your investment portfolio, tax strategy, estate documents, insurance coverage, real estate holdings, cash flow, and retirement timeline are all interconnected — yet most clients find that each professional they work with operates in isolation.

Total Wealth Management is our answer to that problem. It is designed for those who appreciate a concierge, fully integrated approach to financial planning — where a multi-disciplined team at The Affinity Group takes ownership of coordinating every area of your financial life and every professional in your orbit.

Rather than one-off advice on individual topics, our clients experience an ongoing, living strategy that evolves with them — reviewed twice each year, refined continuously, and executed by a dedicated team that knows your entire financial picture as well as you do.

The Process

How a Total Wealth Management Relationship Works

1

Onboarding Process

Discovery Meeting

Who you are and what’s most important to you is where it all begins. Our Senior Wealth Advisor sits down for an in-depth discovery session — understanding your values, goals, concerns, and complete financial picture. Personal financial planning must be truly personal.

Plan Delivery & Specialists

Our team delivers the completed financial plan alongside a hand-curated Summary of Recommendations. Outside specialists in tax planning and estate planning join this meeting to review findings and provide a coordinated second opinion.

Implementation

Within 60 days following the Recommendations Meeting, our team rolls up their sleeves and quarterbacks the implementation of every agreed-upon action item — from account transitions to insurance applications to attorney referrals.

2

Ongoing Relationship

Spring & Fall Reviews

Proper planning is not a single event — it’s an ongoing, never-ending process. Clients receive a full Wealth Plan Review every spring and every fall, with updated recommendations, revised projections, and fresh specialist input.

Unlimited In-Between Access

Life doesn’t wait for scheduled reviews. Your team is always reachable between meetings for questions, major financial decisions, market events, job changes, inheritances, or any situation that requires attention.

Technology & Transparency

As a Registered Investment Advisor serving clients nationwide, we leverage world-class technology to deliver the same personal touch virtually as we do in person. Every client receives full portal access, real-time reporting, and complete transparency on fees and holdings.
