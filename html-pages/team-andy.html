Get to Know Andy

Henton & Associates, Inc. is an accounting firm founded in 2005, owned by CPA and Financial Planner, Andy Henton. Bringing more than 15 years of experience as a CPA and 10 years as a CFP, Andy offers the highest level of expertise in his field. As President of the accounting firm at Henton & Associates, Andy provides unrivaled tax compliance, preparation, planning, and management services for individuals and small businesses.

In his spare time, the graduate of John Carroll University focuses on his wife Elizabeth, his two kids Eleanor and Andrew, playing golf, and coaching his children’s sports teams in Westlake.
