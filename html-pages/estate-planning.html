Estate Planning

“Will, Powers of Attorney, Living Wills are all just the start of evaluating the vision you have for your legacy.”

We evaluate clients’ estate plans and introduce them to a network estate planning attorney to help them ensure their goals will be fully met with their current documents.

Step One

Setting Up the Correct Estate Documents

It takes a lifetime to build a comfortable nest egg for retirement, however many have not fully evaluated exactly what would happen in the event of a premature death. With a couple conversations, a few swipes of the pen, and a few sentences of an estate plan that summarizes all of your wishes and puts in place proper protections for your loved ones can be achieved.

Step Two

Managing Unknown Risk

We find that many of our clients when we first meet them are unknowingly putting their children and loved ones at risk and not even realizing it by leaving assets to them outright.

We walk through all the important considerations as it relates to each client’s specific familial situation.

Step Three

Taking Care of Everything

We then conduct a state alignment across all of the client’s assets and financial accounts, meaning once the proper estate planning documents are put in place they directly help the client get account titling correct and proper beneficiaries updated on all their financial accounts and assets.

Our Estate Planning Services

Personalized Estate Planning

Our team takes a personalized approach to craft tailored strategies that align with your unique financial goals, family dynamics, and legacy aspirations.

Will & Trust Formation

We guide you through will and trust formation — revocable living trusts, irrevocable trusts, or comprehensive wills — ensuring your intentions are legally sound.

Probate Avoidance

We craft tailored strategies that align with your unique financial goals, family dynamics, and legacy aspirations to keep your estate out of probate.

Tax-Efficient Planning

We guide you through strategies that minimize estate and income taxes, preserving more of your wealth for the people and causes you care about.

Business Succession Planning

We create personalized strategies for transferring business ownership that protect your legacy and ensure a smooth transition for all parties involved.

Charitable Giving Strategies

We help you articulate your philanthropic wishes clearly and implement giving vehicles that reduce taxes while supporting the causes you believe in.

Family Wealth Education

We take a personalized approach to prepare the next generation to be responsible stewards of the wealth you’ve worked a lifetime to build.

Guardianship & Conservatorship
