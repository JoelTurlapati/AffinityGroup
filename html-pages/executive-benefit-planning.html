Executive Benefit Planning

Executive benefits enable an organization to selectively reward the key employees and executives of a business. Unlike qualified plans like 401(k) plans, there are no coverage or participation requirements for an executive benefit program. This allows a company to provide rewards and incentives on an employee-by-employee basis, offering maximum design flexibility.

A well-designed executive benefit program can provide incentives that help retain key executives for the longest possible time and help companies compete to attract top talent who will contribute to company growth and profitability.

Why It Matters

Attracting & Retaining Top Talent

Executive benefits help companies compete and attract key executives who will contribute to company growth and profitability. A well-designed program can provide incentives that help retain key executives for the longest possible time.

Because these plans fall outside qualified plan rules, they offer unmatched flexibility to customize rewards based on individual executive value and strategic importance to the organization.
