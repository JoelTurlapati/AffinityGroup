The Reality

Founding a Business Is One of the Greatest Accomplishments — and One of the Greatest Financial Risks.

Founding a successful and thriving business is one of the greatest accomplishments a business owner can achieve. It will also be one of their greatest assets. However, owners may find that the demands of the business dictate their personal lives.

For most business owners, wealth is concentrated in a single asset — the business itself — leaving personal finances, estate plans, and retirement goals perpetually on the backburner.
