Get to Know Mark

Mark focuses primarily on helping clients with estate planning, trusts, as well as estate & business succession planning. He has implemented many sophisticated estate plans involving individuals at all ranges of personal net worth.

His practice includes preparing and serving as trustee and charitable lead and remainder trusts, private foundations, complex multi-generation trusts, marital deduction, and “by-pass” trusts.

He has frequently served as an expert witness in trust and estate matters, including conflicts of interest among multiple clients, legal malpractice in estate planning, trustee distribution issues, and trustee investment diversification.

Mark is a frequent speaker on estate planning topics sponsored by various professional organizations and financial institutions. Notably, he has been a co-editor of Ohio Transaction Guide, published by Matthew Bender & Co. since 1996. Mark belongs to the Cleveland Metropolitan Bar Association and is also a member of the honorary society, Beta Alpha Psi.

Mark received his Juris Doctor Degree from Case Western Reserve School of Law in 1988. He graduated cum laude with a Bachelor of Science Degree in 1985.
