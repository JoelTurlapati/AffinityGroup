Affinity Private Tax is not a public tax-preparation shop. This is a private, ongoing planning relationship for individuals and families connected to Affinity Wealth.

This Service Is Designed For:

  • Affinity Wealth Total Wealth Management clients

Retirement Tax Strategies

Retirement Tax Integration

Retirement creates new and complex tax planning opportunities. The decisions made before and during retirement around distributions, conversions, Social Security, and estate transfers all affect the long-term tax picture.

Roth Conversion Planning

Identify conversion windows in the years before RMDs begin and model multi-year strategies to reduce future taxable income.

Required Minimum Distributions

Plan RMD timing, qualified charitable distributions, and coordination with other income sources to manage tax liability.

Social Security Optimization

Review the tax treatment of Social Security benefits and coordinate the timing of other income to minimize taxation.

Income Sequencing & Gap Years

Plan which accounts to draw from and in what order during early retirement when income is lower and conversion opportunities exist.

Medicare Premium Planning

Review IRMAA thresholds and plan income to avoid unexpected Medicare surcharges tied to prior-year income.

Estate & Inherited IRA Planning

Coordinate estate transfer strategies and plan for inherited IRA distributions with tax efficiency in mind.
