Who Affinity Private Tax Serves

Affinity Private Tax is not a standalone tax preparation service for the general public. We work with clients where tax preparation and tax planning are part of a larger advisory relationship.

Total Wealth Management Clients

Individuals & Families

Individuals, families, retirees, executives, and business owners who are engaged in Affinity Wealth’s Total Wealth Management relationship receive coordinated tax planning and preparation as part of their integrated advisory experience.

Outsourced CFO Business Clients

Business Owners

Business-owner clients who utilize our Outsourced CFO service and want coordinated bookkeeping, business tax preparation, and owner-level tax planning — all managed through one integrated relationship.

We do not provide tax preparation for:

  • Individuals who are not Affinity Wealth Total Wealth Management clients
  • Businesses that are not part of our Outsourced CFO relationship
  • Clients seeking only a one-time tax return
  • Clients who are not looking for integrated planning
  • Businesses with incomplete or unsupported books (unless a cleanup engagement is first completed)

This allows our team to remain focused on the clients we are built to serve: families and business owners who want coordinated advice across their full financial picture.

Ongoing oversight from professionals with bookkeeping, accounting, and tax-planning experience

Why It Matters

The goal is not simply accurate books. The goal is books that support better tax planning, cleaner tax preparation, stronger cash-flow management, and more informed business decisions throughout the year.

Because Bionic Bookkeeping™ is reviewed through both a bookkeeping and tax-planning lens, transactions are categorized with greater attention to how they may impact deductions, tax strategy, and financial reporting.

This integrated approach helps reduce errors, minimize missed opportunities, and keep your books tax-ready year-round. With accurate, up-to-date financial records, our team can provide more effective tax planning guidance, better cash-flow insights, and a stronger foundation for long-term decision-making.
